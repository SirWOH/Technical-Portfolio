About Me


From a young age I always had knack for technology. Whenever my parents bought a new phone or computer, I was tasked with learning how to use it and teaching everyone in the house. It got to the point where I took a part a few computers and put them back together for fun in High School. I graduated from Elms College with a bachelor in Computers Information Technology (CIT), and then came back to Boston to help out my family. I then went into sales for a couple of years and completed a 14 – week intense coding boot camp learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React and Node.js. Now I’m ready to take the knowledge that I acquired and help clients with the development of their website and customer acquisition and lead generation.
